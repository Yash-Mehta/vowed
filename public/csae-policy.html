Child Safety Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Our Commitment

Vowed has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form. We are committed to protecting minors from harm and ensuring our platform is never used to produce, distribute, or facilitate access to child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that exploits or endangers children.

Platform Context

Vowed is a private, invitation-only social app designed exclusively for wedding events. Access requires a unique invite code issued by a verified wedding host. All members are adults participating in a specific wedding event. The app is not intended for use by minors.

Prohibited Content and Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited on Vowed:

  • Any content that sexually exploits or endangers minors
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Grooming, solicitation, or trafficking of minors
  • Any content that sexualizes individuals under the age of 18

Enforcement

Any user found violating this policy will be immediately removed from the platform. All confirmed CSAM will be reported to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement authorities in accordance with applicable law.

Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior on Vowed that violates this policy, please report it immediately by contacting us at safety@vowed.app. All reports are reviewed promptly and treated with the utmost seriousness.

Standards Alignment

Vowed's child safety practices align with the guidelines set forth by the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and comply with the PROTECT Our Children Act and applicable international standards for online child safety.
